FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment anytime using our online tracking tool available on the website. Simply enter your tracking number for real-time updates.

What shipping services do you offer?

We provide a full range of logistics services including international and domestic shipping, cargo freight, pet delivery, e-commerce logistics, customs clearance, warehousing, and more.

How long does international shipping take?

Delivery times vary by destination and shipping method. Typically, international shipments take between 3 to 5 business days. Contact us for specific estimates.

Do you provide insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer cargo insurance options to protect your goods against loss or damage during transit. Please contact our support team for details.

How can I request a shipping quote?

Use the “Get a Quote” form on our website or contact our customer service team with your shipment details for a personalized estimate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, and online payment platforms. Payment terms will be specified in your invoice.
